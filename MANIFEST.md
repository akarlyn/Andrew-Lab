## Mission

Provide a deliberately public environment for testing and publishing generalizable methods, prompts, agents, workflows, and research that do not expose private Executive Digital Twin content.

| Area | Purpose | Stage 0 status |

## Prohibited content classes

- Private Executive Digital Twin content
- Confidential client or partner information
- Relationship intelligence
- Proprietary proposal or capture assets
- Credentials and secrets
- Personal data that is not intentionally public
- Restricted datasets
- Unlicensed third-party content

## Graduation paths

- **Lab to Andrew OS:** Private learning, validated reasoning, or intellectual capital is re-created privately, not copied with confidential context.
- **Lab to Kindora OS:** Operational methods are transferred only after review and adaptation for private use.
- **Andrew OS or Kindora OS to Lab:** Assets require an explicit public-release review before publication.

## Current gaps

- No repository license has been selected.

- No experiments have been registered.
- No publication-review owner beyond Andrew has been designated.
